Adaptive Questions

Our personalisation algorithm works hard to pick up your weak concepts through the practice questions attempted. Find yourself wonky in a certain area? Our algorithm singles out the concepts you might not have grasped well in. Subsequent sets of questions are specially curated for you to tackle them and solidify your basic foundation.



Insightful Analytics

Get meaningful insights to your understanding with our progress reports. How ready are you to tackle those questions in an exam setting? Our progress report analyses where you are in mastering a certain concept, whether you are still grappling with the fundamentals or whether you are ready to excel under time pressure with our inter-topic practices.



Peer Learning

We believe in collaborative learning. Post your questions in the discussion forum and get help. At the same time, expose yourself to challenging questions from your peers.



Clarify Doubts Anytime

Our tutors are always available to help you. Encountered a difficult question outside of class time? Post your questions on our discussion board and our tutors will answer them as soon as possible.
