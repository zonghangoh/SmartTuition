Blended Learning noun

  /ˌblendɪd ˈlɜːnɪŋ/

A style of education in which students learn via electronic and online media as well as traditional face-to-face teaching.

"Modern technology has expanded the possibilities of how teachers can engage students to learn better, but no one does personalisation and blended learning quite as effectively as Smart Tuition can!"


We do not believe in one-size-fits-all.

Instead, we believe in learning at your own pace. A perfect collaboration between our automated learning system and experienced tutors, we deliver just what our students need.



How It Works


During Class

Learn Key Concepts
Tutors will go through the key concepts of the selected topic at the beginning of every lesson.

Practice
Students will attempt the standardised set of questions on the Super app while tutors will go around the class to address questions that the students may have.

1-1 Consultation
Based one the progress reports generated by our systems, tutors will have a one-to-one consultation with every student to provide more help on the concepts that they are weaker in.

After Class

Personalised Practice Questions
We encourage continued learning even beyond our classes. Post-lesson, students try more questions on their own on our Super App, where learning pace is personalised for each student.

Our algorithms also heed the best pedagogical practices to optimise learning. 

Monitor Learning Progress
A topical progress report is generated at the end of each lesson, refreshed with every additional practice attempt. Viewable by both tutors and students, we make progress tracking easy.
