How It Works


During Class

Learn Key Concepts
Tutors will go through the key concepts of the selected topic at the beginning of every lesson.

Practice
Students will attempt the standardised set of questions on the Smarty app while tutors will go around the class to address questions that the students may have.

1-1 Consultation
Based one the progress reports generated by our systems, tutors will have a one-to-one consultation with every student to provide more help on the concepts that they are weaker in.

After Class

Personalised Practice Questions
Post-lesson, students get to try more questions on their own. With each set of practice, our algorithms pick up weaker concepts and generate a more curated set of questions to strengthen learning outcomes.

Our algorithms also heed the best pedagogical practices such as Practice Testing, Interleaved Practice and Spatial Practice to optimise learning and memory retention.

Monitor Learning Progress
A progress report on each topic will be generated at the end of the lesson, and will be updated after every additional attempt on the practice questions. It will be viewable by both tutors and students to keep track of learning outcomes.
