H2 Math

H2 math is a challenging but fulfilling subject to take on. The key to mastering H2 Math is about preparation, finding out what you don’t know, and building a solid foundation in those areas.
H2 Math requires students to be more proactive in bridging knowledge gaps. More often than not, students struggle filling in the knowledge gaps, because many lecturers in school tend to breeze through concepts and when students finally attempt the questions, the knowledge cracks start to appear. Smarty and our tutors aim to tackle just that: finding the gaps in your concepts in the most efficient way possible and bridging them.
It is important that you start early in getting help to solidify your foundation. So, quit delaying and get help from our experienced tutors and benefit from our proven pedagogy before it is too late!

• H2 Physics

The H2 Physics syllabus does not only require students to recall the factual formulas, but also to apply concepts in a logical, reasoned manner to higher order thinking questions.
Instead of blindly drilling question after question, students will be able to save so much more time with us. With practice questions from Smarty, students will be able to pinpoint whether they are able to apply a particular concept into questions. Our experienced tutors will then zero into weaker topics and help understand how concepts are interlinked.

H2 Biology

Biology can be a tricky subject to master, due to the amount of content. With the sheer amount of information, many still find themselves struggling even though they have “memorised” the notes by heart.
At Smart Tuition, our tutors believe in correct strategies to tackle the questions, rather than rote learning. Our algorithm is configured to identify the keywords you will need to score in exam questions. Students will be assessed accurately on their understanding of their topic with algorithm marking. With that, our tutors will also guide students on how to capture the essence of questions, so that they can score strategically.

H2 Chemistry

H2 Chemistry can be one of the most challenging subjects for students. It requires students to not only memorise, but also understand how chemical formulas are derived and apply the gist of every concept.
Our tutors aim to break down the concepts in a succinct manner, and solidify their understanding with Practice Testing on Smarty. When students are able to showcase a strong foundation, our algorithm will present more stimulating questions with Interleaved Practices to test the linkages between topics. With a combination of curated questions and tutors’ coaching on application of concepts across topics, our pedagogy has proven to bring the ‘A’ grade for our students.
