Elementary Maths

E-Maths is an unchallenging, yet an intricate subject. At Smart Tuition, our programme structure is designed to make learning Mathematics easier and enjoyable. Tailored to meet your learning needs, identify concept gaps with our app and get them corrected with our experienced tutors. Strengthen your foundations with us now.


Additional Mathematics

A-Math requires a higher level of thinking and analytical skills than E-Maths. Students need to have a more in-depth understanding of the mathematical concepts. At Smart Tuition, we provide a wide range of practice questions, curated to your learning needs. Learn the A* strategies to present answers and tackle exam questions efficiently from our A* tutors.


Pure Chemistry

Chemistry is a challenging subject, requiring a deep understanding of mathematics and a strong foundation in fundamental concepts. The Super App exposes our students to a variety of question types and trains them perfectly to understand linkages between basic Chemistry concepts. Build your confidence in Chemistry with us before it is too late.


Pure Biology

There are numerous concepts covered in Biology and many students find it difficult to memorise all of them. Our curriculum focuses on strengthening students' understanding of concepts taught through collaborative learning and constant practice, allowing them to retain the information in their mind for a longer period of time.


Pure Physics

Physics is not all about memorising formulas. Instead, we focus on precise application of concepts in our questions and coaching. Our tutors guide students to identify concepts needed to tackle each question, rather than blindly applying formulas. Improve your application skills with tons of curated practice questions and tackle your exams confidently.
