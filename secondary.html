Additional Mathematics

A-Math is a highly valuable learning experience as it sets the foundation for higher-level mathematics in JC and Polytechnics. Compared to E-Math, A-Math is more complex and requires a higher level of thinking and analytical skills. Students need to have a more in-depth understanding of the mathematical concepts and be extra careful as the questions usually involve more workings.
The key to scoring this subject is practice. We provide a wide range of practice questions on Smarty to familiarise students with the common exam questions. Assessing the performance after every practice, additional sets of questions will be curated for students to work on concepts that they are weaker in. In addition, students will learn strategies to present answers and tackle exam questions efficiently to score in exams.
